Dataset description: Dataset: 2016 Make Model Year Failures at NCT Issued By: The Road Safety Authority Information relating to the important features such as VehicleMake, YearofBirth, Total, PASS, PASS % and FAIL, FAIL% are provided. Its a big data set containing 8000+ entries of Vehicles tested by the NCT in the year 2016.
  • NCT Data 2016

  • VehicleMake - Represents the which company has manufactured the car.
  • VehicleModel - Represents the model of the car company has manufactured.
  • YearOfBirth - Represents the year of manufacture.
  • Total - Represents the total number of NCT test cases.
  • Pass - Represents the number of NCT Test cases passed.
  • Pass - Represents the number of NCT Test cases failed.
Initial questions
  • How each NCT fail items have changed over the years?
  • What are the similarities and differences between each Fail item?
Description:  The series of charts shows the trend of each NCT fail item over the years. Each of the fail item are plotted against the Year of Manufacture.
Insight: This series of graphs shows that most of the fail items have followed the same trend over years with exceptions. The shape of the area is almost similar to each other except Emmissions, Suspension, Others and Incompletables. Year's where the trend reaches the peak are marked.
Design considerations Idea here is to compare each fail item with each other and to find the similarities and differences over time. To plot a trend against Time, I have used a area plot. Alternatively, a line plot can also be used. When the number of plots increase, it's hard to find the differences in case of line plot. This problem can be solved when it's a area graph. The area is filled with blue color which increases the readability and gives the power to distinguish between the graphs for the user.
Data filtering and transformation:  Firstly, each fail item is grouped over years. Secondly, the sum of fail item for each year is calculated. Lastly, the series of each fail item against the years are plotted.
